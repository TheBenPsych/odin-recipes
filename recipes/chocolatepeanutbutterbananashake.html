Ben's Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Shake Recipe

Description

There is nothing quite like a nice refreshing breakfast shake. This one will throw you for a loop when you wake up wondering why you never tried it before. With chocolate and peanut butter you know it will be good, but adding peanut butter too? Get ready for this one.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients

  • Banana - 1 medium
  • Lowfat milk - 1 cup
  • Ice cubes 3/4 cup
  • Peanut butter 1/4 cup
  • Cocoa powder - 2 tbsp
  • Vanilla extract - 1/2 tsp

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and pulse until smooth.
  2. Enjoy!
