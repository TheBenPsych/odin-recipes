Ben's Banana Almond Butter and Raisins Recipe

Description

We don't always have a lot of time to wip up some quality food or even something healthy, but here is one that proves that wrong! This combination of bananas, almonds and raisins will have you excited to eat.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients

  • Raisins - 5 raisins
  • Banana - 1 medium
  • Almond butter - 2 tsp

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Steps

  1. Slice ripe banana lengthwise.
  2. Spread with 2 tsp creamy natural almond butter.
  3. Dot the top with 5 raisins.
  4. Enjoy!
