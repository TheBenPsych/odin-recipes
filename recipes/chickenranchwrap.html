Ben's Chicken Ranch Wrap Recipe

Description

Chicken ranch wraps have all of the spiritual goodness we crave. Lovely tender and juicy chicken, a pinch of what rabbits eat to kinda make it healthy and taste fresh. Last but certainly not least, some nice creamy ranch to bring it all together.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients

  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp
  • Chicken breast - 20oz
  • Ranch dressing - 2 tbsp
  • Tortilla - 1 7"-8"
  • Shredded Lettuce and or Spinach - 1 cups total

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Steps

  1. Chop chicken into bite sized pieces. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Cook chicken for 10-15 minutes, or until cooked through, no longer pink, and the juices run clear. Set aside.
  2. Toss chicken with the ranch dressing until coated.
  3. To make a wrap, spread a half a cup of the chicken mixture on a wrap and top with a cup of lettuce and or spinach.
  4. Wrap can be heated up in microwave slightly to make soft; heat up the mixture as well for a hot sandwich.
  5. Slam it into your mouth and enjoy.
